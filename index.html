TalenGen Internship Opportunities

Job Title: Project-Based Software Engineer (Live Chat Feature)

  • Location : Online
  • Job Type: Project-based internship

About TalenGen:

TalenGen is a rapidly growing startup aiming to become the #1 recruitment platform in Southeast Asia (SEA). We provide a comprehensive suite of networking, job recruiting, live chat, AI matching, and events features on our platform. Our mission is to connect talented individuals with top-notch opportunities and streamline the hiring process for both employers and job seekers.

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with the team to understand the requirements and desired functionalities of the Live Chat feature.
  • Design, develop, test, and deploy chat functionality using appropriate technologies and frameworks.
  • Implement real-time messaging capabilities, including text-based communication, file sharing, and message notifications.
  • Ensure smooth integration of the Live Chat feature with other platform components.
  • Enhance user experience by implementing features such as message threading, chat history, and user presence indicators.
  • Optimize chat performance and scalability to support a large user base.
  • Work closely with UI/UX designers to create an intuitive and visually appealing chat interface.
  • Collaborate with the testing team to conduct thorough testing and debugging of the chat feature.
  • Document technical specifications, code, and development processes for future reference.
  • Regularly report project progress to the CTO, including status updates, milestones achieved, challenges faced, and proposed solutions.
  • Actively participate in code reviews, providing constructive feedback and suggestions for improvement.

Qualifications:

  • Currently pursuing a Bachelor's or higher degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
  • Strong understanding of software development principles, data structures, and algorithms.
  • Proficiency in at least one of this programming language/framework - JavaScript, React, Node.js, WebSocket, etc
  • Familiarity with real-time communication protocols and techniques.
  • Knowledge of web application development and RESTful API integration.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work independently as well as collaboratively in a team.
  • Excellent communication skills and the ability to effectively articulate technical concepts.
  • Ability to provide clear and concise project updates to the CTO, highlighting progress, milestones, challenges, and proposed solutions.

Nice to have:

  • Previous experience with chat or messaging systems.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or GCP.
  • Understanding of user authentication and authorization mechanisms.
  • Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git) and agile development methodologies.

Joining TalenGen offers an exciting opportunity to work in a dynamic and fast-paced startup environment, shaping the future of recruitment in SEA. If you are passionate about building innovative software solutions and want to make a significant impact, we would love to hear from you.

To apply, please send your resume and a cover letter highlighting your relevant experience and why you are interested in joining TalenGen to info@talengen.com
